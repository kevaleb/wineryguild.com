About Us

We Believe

Wine should NOT be intimidating

It should be relatable, accessible, Easy, etc.

Quality wine comes at every price point

Price/brand are not necessarily the prime indicators of quality.

Wine connects people

We want to connect consumers to the people who make the wine. Differentiation Ideas Process Beliefs

Our Mission

  • We want to change the way people think about wine (fun not intimidating)
  • We want to be the best place to discover new great wines
  • We want to be the best place to get to know the people who make your wine
  • We want to simplify the wine world for the “average, lay” wine drinker.
  • Quality and affordability

What we do

  • Visit wineries
  • Share their stories
  • Identify the best wines
  • Make them available to everyone (except those poor bastards who live in places with outdated wine shipping laws)
