About Us

We Believe

  • Wine can be a quality drinking experience at any price point.
  • Enjoying wine involves more than just the palate.
  • Wine should be explored without intimidation by popular opinion.
  • Being acquainted with the story behind the wine creates a better drinking experience.


Our Mission

  • Change the way people think about wine
  • Be the best place to discover new great wines
  • Be THE place to bring wine makers and customers together in a mutually beneficial relationship.
  • Simplify the wine world for the “ lay” wine drinker.
  • Bring quality and affordability to your drinking experience


What we do

  • Expose you to out of the way, little known wines.
  • Visit wineries
  • Share their stories
  • Identify the best wines
  • Make them available to everyone (except those poor bastards who live in places with outdated wine shipping laws)
